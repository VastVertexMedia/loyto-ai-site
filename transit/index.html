Vehicle Asset Management

Every vehicle.
From purchase to disposal.

Transit Assistant manages the complete lifecycle of every vehicle in your fleet — not just where it is today, but its full history from the day it was acquired to the day it leaves service.

Every maintenance record, every odometer reading, every service event, and every status change is captured and traceable — giving your agency a single source of truth for fleet management and compliance documentation.

🛒
Purchase & Registration Vehicle acquisition details, purchase price, registration, and funding source recorded at entry — linked to the grant that funded it.
✅
In-Service Activation Vehicle placed into active service with initial odometer reading recorded — establishing the baseline for all future mileage and utilization tracking.
🔧
Scheduled Maintenance Maintenance reminders triggered automatically based on mileage and service intervals — keeping your fleet compliant and reducing unexpected downtime.
📋
Maintenance Records Every service event recorded with date, mileage, work performed, and cost — building a complete and auditable maintenance history for every vehicle.
⏸️
Out-of-Service & Return Vehicles removed from and returned to service tracked with timestamps and reason codes — maintaining accurate fleet availability at all times.
📤
Disposal & Retirement Final odometer reading, disposal method, and documentation recorded at end of life — completing the vehicle record and supporting FTA disposal reporting requirements.

Grant Management

Every grant. Every budget.
Every dollar — accounted for.

Transit Assistant manages the full financial lifecycle of your agency's federal and state grants — from budget setup through expense tracking — with each grant type managed according to its own specific line item structure.

📁
Grant Setup & Configuration
Per grant type

Each grant is configured with its own budget structure and line items — because Section 5311, Section 5310, Section 5307, and state grants all have different reporting requirements and different allowable expense categories. Transit Assistant handles each one on its own terms.

5311 configuration 5310 configuration 5307 configuration State grant support Custom line items
💰
Budget Management
Line item level

Budgets are tracked at the line item level — not just the grant total. Your team sees exactly how much has been allocated, how much has been spent, and how much remains on each individual budget line — across every active grant simultaneously.

Line item budgets Multi-grant view Remaining balance Budget vs. actual
🧾
Expense Tracking
Against grant line items

Expenses are recorded and mapped directly against the appropriate grant and line item — keeping every dollar traceable to its funding source. Auditors and program managers always see exactly where the money went and which grant it came from.

Expense entry Line item mapping Funding source trace Audit-ready records
📊
Grant Reporting
Always current

Grant status reports — budget, expenses, remaining balance, and utilization rate — are generated from live data at any time. Your agency is always ready for a program review, an audit, or a grant amendment — not just at reporting deadlines.

Status reports Utilization rate Amendment support Audit documentation
🔔
Budget Alert System

Transit Assistant monitors grant budgets continuously — alerting your team when spending on any line item approaches its budget limit. Your program managers get advance notice before a line item is exhausted, not a surprise after the fact. Every grant, every line, every dollar — always under control.
